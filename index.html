About Kieran Dunch Using soft skills and technical expertise to make web development accessible for all.

Get to know Kieran:

Kieran Dunch is a Full stack web developer located in North America. He has a passion for creating beautiful, responsive and accessible websites and applications. He loves to use communication and teaching skills he honed as a theatre artist to help his clients understand software.

His work as a freelancer has allowed him to work as a product owner and front-end engineer for a variety of clients. Beside software, he loves surfing, rock climbing, and learning new things.
